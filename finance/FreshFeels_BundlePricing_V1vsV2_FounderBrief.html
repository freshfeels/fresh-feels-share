1 Executive Summary

We are proposing to relaunch the Fresh Start Bundle at $79.90 inc GST (V2), replacing the current $66.25 price point (V1). The change adds two strategically chosen products to the bundle — a second Multipurpose Spray Bottle and a single pack of Travel Hand Wash Sheets — lifting the combined individual RRP from $132.50 to $162.30. The "50%+ off" positioning is maintained and is in fact stronger than before (50.7% off vs exactly 50%).

2 Background & Context

Why we are reviewing bundle pricing

Fresh Feels absorbs approximately $12 in outbound shipping on every order above $60 inc GST. With the current $66.25 bundle this shipping cost eats a material portion of margin. Our break-even model — built from actual Xero P&L data using $25,018 total monthly fixed costs (including $6,270 in marketing) — shows we need to generate nearly 882 orders every single month before we are profitable.

At current acquisition costs and organic traffic, that volume is achievable but leaves almost no buffer. Any month with a traffic dip, a returns spike, or an ad-cost increase tips us into the red. We need a more robust per-order margin, and we need the acquisition product to do more work for the business beyond the first order.

The shipping absorption policy

We free-ship all orders $60 inc GST and above. On sub-$60 orders the customer pays shipping. This means:

  • Every Fresh Start Bundle order (V1 $66.25, V2 $79.90) absorbs $12 shipping — this is the core margin pressure.
  • Single-item sheet orders ($9.90–$33.90) typically do not trigger free shipping, so their CM% is naturally higher.
  • Raising the bundle price does not trigger any extra shipping cost — we absorb $12 regardless at either price point.

Why these two products?

  • Second Spray Bottle — the repurchase accelerator: with one bottle, the customer typically sets up in a single room (usually the kitchen) and consumes Multipurpose Cleaning Sheets at a single-room rate. A second bottle means kitchen and bathroom running simultaneously — doubling the rate at which they go through the sheet packs and pulling forward the reorder trigger. The COGS is only $1.97 yet the RRP is $19.90, making it the most margin-efficient item we could add. It also genuinely completes the "whole home starter kit" story — the customer leaves with everything they need to set up two rooms from day one.
  • Travel Hand Wash Sheets — category introduction: unlisted as an individual product at $9.90 (the 3-pack sells at $26.90, making a single-pack price conservative and credible). It extends the Fresh Feels story beyond cleaning into personal care and opens a second repurchase stream that isn't connected to the kitchen or laundry cycle at all. COGS is $1.52. Low risk, high narrative value.
  • Combined efficiency: together these two additions add $29.80 to the individual RRP but only $3.49 to total COGS — the best possible additions from a margin standpoint.
Strategic note on the multipurpose sheets: one pack makes over 20 bottles of spray, so customers don't repurchase quickly even with one bottle. The second bottle isn't just a value-add — it's an intentional mechanism to accelerate consumption and shorten the repurchase cycle on our highest-volume consumable.

6 Customer Value Positioning

The critical constraint on pricing is maintaining the "50%+ off individual RRP" claim legitimately. Under V2:

  • Combined individual RRP = $162.30
  • Bundle price = $79.90
  • Saving = $82.40, which is 50.7% off — more than 50%, so the claim is stronger than V1.

Recommended messaging framework

What to avoid Do NOT use "free gift" language for the second bottle or hand wash sheets — the price increase accounts for both additions, so calling anything "free" is misleading and legally risky under Australian Consumer Law.

8 Recommendation

Proceed with V2 — the $79.90 bundle — subject to the following pre-launch checklist:

  • Pre-launch Update the Shopify product listing: title, description, product images, and price from $66.25 to $79.90. Update item count from 5 to 7.
  • Pre-launch Audit all live Meta/Google ad creatives for $66.25 and "5 products" references — pause or update before launch.
  • Pre-launch Update email flows (welcome sequence, abandoned cart, post-purchase) with new bundle value messaging and product count.
  • Pre-launch Brief fulfilment team on the updated 7-product pick list. Confirm Travel Hand Wash Sheets (single) are in stock and correctly slotted.
  • Post-launch Set a 14-day post-launch review of conversion rate and AOV versus the V1 baseline.
  • Post-launch If conversion rate drops more than 15% week-on-week, convene a review before committing further ad spend at the new price.
  • Post-launch At 60 days, check if repurchase rate on Multipurpose Cleaning Sheets has lifted — the second bottle should be visible in the data.
Note on the Travel Hand Wash Sheets SKU: this product is currently unlisted on Shopify at $9.90 individual. Confirm the SKU is set up correctly in the system and that stock is available before the bundle goes live.
